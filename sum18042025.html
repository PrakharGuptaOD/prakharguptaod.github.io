Workflow to Create Memes

  1. Curate Clips: Assign a creative team member who is familiar with memes and reels to identify suitable video clips.
  2. Generate Captions: Draft a detailed prompt describing the video content and use ChatGPT (with chain-of-thought enabled) to craft a caption that conveys the intended message.
  3. Edit the Meme: Combine the video clip and generated caption—crediting the original creator—using editors like CapCut, Canva, Veed.io, or InShot.
  4. Publish the Meme: Review the final meme to ensure it remains appropriate before posting.

Platforms to Target

The memes will be primarily shared on LinkedIn, X (formerly Twitter), and YouTube to promote the ARPIT program. While Instagram will also host content, higher-education faculty—the main audience—rarely use Instagram for learning.

Animation Series Plans

We propose an animated series featuring:

  • Narrator: Personification of the ARPIT program.
  • Characters: Chintu, Laddoo, and Mr. Chatur—a seasoned teacher yet to integrate technology.

The storyline follows Mr. Chatur’s journey of adopting tech-enhanced teaching, guided by the Narrator. If the pilot succeeds, we will expand the production team.
